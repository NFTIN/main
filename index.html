Know NFTIN (Non-fungible Token)?

A non-fungible token (NFTIN) is a digital file whose unique identity and ownership are verified on a blockchain (a digital ledger). NFTIN are not mutually interchangeable. NFTIN are commonly created by uploading files, such as digital artwork, to an auction market.
